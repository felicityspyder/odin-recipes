Hummus Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (15-ounce) can chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 large lemon
  • 1/4 cup well-sirred tahini
  • 1 small garlic clove minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  1. Blend tahini and lemon juice together in a food processor or blender for 1-2 minutes
  2. Add minced garlic and olive oil and mix for an additional 30 seconds
  3. Finally add chickpeas and blend on low. May have to additionally stir mixture by hand to ensure consistency throughout.
  4. ENJOY!
