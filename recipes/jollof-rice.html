Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1/4 cup of oil
  • a thumbsize piece of ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 5 Tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 2 cups parboiled (or long grain rice)
  • 1 1/3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 Tablespoon curry powder and thyme

Ingredients: Sauce Base

  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1 onion

Instructions

  1. Fryonions in a saucepan with the oil
  2. Addginger, garlic, and bay leaves
  3. After cooking for 2-3 minutes, add the tomato paste and cook on the stovetop for an additional 3-4 minutes
  4. Blendtogether the red pepper, tomatoes, chili, and onion in a blender. Add the sauce base and let simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Stir-inthe rice, vegetable stock, and spices
  6. Simmeron low heat for 15-20 minutes
